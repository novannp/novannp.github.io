Social Media Solving

A social media crisis is an unexpected event that results in an extraordinarily high volume of negative conversation about the brand in a short time period. There are different types of crises. Some of them can be prepared for or addressed early. Others will spring up out of the blue with little or no warning.

Listening

Media listening is the process of "listening in cyberspace" as information that may become an issue
in online media, by using a system, which runs by expert people.

Using 1 (one) keyword will be used as material media listening.

Report product in the form of screening results topics or issues from multiple platforms matches the listening container.

Reports in real time whenever found the main issue or topic that considered a potential crisis. As well as being an analysis material for used as media monitoring.

Monitoring

Monitoring is the process of “reading, viewing” editorial content of sources from online media (social media and online news portals), listening to media results, especially content containing certain keywords or topics, as well as collecting data that occurs in online media based on social aspects. then processed, identified and analyzed. So as to be able to detect and anticipate early content according to the results of monitoring. Furthermore, they are able to provide recommendations to clients for making policies and decisions.

Tracking

Tracking is a way to identify social network of an account, personal, or organization related to an issue.
We are is done using systems and conventional methods to produce high data accuracy.

Profiling

Profiling is a way to identify a profile from various social media and related platforms to detail a person and combine all the information to reach a conclusion about the involvement of a person or organization in an issue.

Post Activity

Post Activity is a way to carry out a movement or activity on social media or media platforms to be able to raise or lead opinions on a topic or issue.
